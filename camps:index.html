QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our football training camps.

How fit do I need to be to attend a camp? +

Our camps accommodate various fitness levels. We recommend being able to comfortably play a full match, but we design our sessions with appropriate intensity and rest periods for adult players. Each camp description specifies the physical demands, and we offer modifications when needed.

What should I bring to a camp? +

You should bring your normal football gear: boots (both firm ground and turf if possible), shin guards, training and match kit, water bottle, sunscreen, and weather-appropriate clothing. For multi-day camps, we'll provide a detailed packing list after registration.

Are accommodations included in multi-day camps? +

For our intensive retreats, accommodation is typically included in the price. For weekend camps, accommodation is usually not included, but we offer preferred rates at partner hotels. Check the specific camp details or contact us for more information about your chosen camp.

What's the typical coach-to-player ratio? +

We maintain a low coach-to-player ratio to ensure quality instruction. Typically, we have one coach for every 6-8 players. Specialized camps, like goalkeeper training, may have even smaller ratios of 1:4. This allows for personalized attention and feedback.

Can I get a refund if I need to cancel? +

We offer a tiered refund policy: full refund if canceled 30+ days before the camp, 50% refund if canceled 14-29 days before, and no refund for cancellations less than 14 days before. However, we do allow you to transfer your registration to another person or to a future camp (subject to availability).
